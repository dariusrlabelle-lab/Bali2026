Canggu Family Guide

Things To
Do & Explore

Everything you need for eight days in Bali — curated for family, adventure, and total relaxation. From toddler mornings to spa afternoons, late-night dinners to sunrise beaches.

⭐ Must-Do

Mai Main Playground

⏱ 8am–8pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.8

The best toddler destination in Canggu. Foam party daily at 10am, indoor zip line, ball pit, spiral slides, animals, train rides, kids pool and a solid restaurant. Bring socks — required inside.

maimain.com →

Parklife Bali

⏱ 8am–8pm📍 10 min⭐ 4.6

Family café and play park with indoor toddler zone, outdoor play, crafts and staff-run activities every hour. Rice field views from the restaurant. Lets parents actually sit and have a coffee.

parklifebali.com →

Smiley Playhouse

⏱ 9am–8pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.5

Small, cosy, beautifully designed indoor play space. New toys, clean and gentle. Ideal for rainy mornings or low-key afternoons. Perfect for a 2.5 year old.

Family Favourite

Happy Puppy Yoga

📍 12 min⭐ 4.9

Gentle yoga class with adorable shelter puppies roaming the mats. Pure therapy and joy — your daughter will love the puppies. Set by the river and rice fields. A one-off experience not to miss.

happypuppyyogabali.com →

Batu Bolong Beach

🌅 Best before 10am📍 8 min

Closest beach to the villa. Great for early morning walks and paddling with your daughter before the heat peaks. Dark volcanic sand, warm water, relaxed vibe.

La Brisa Beach Club

⏱ 10am–11pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.6

Beautiful bohemian beach club that welcomes families in the daytime. Sophisticated crowd, ocean views, great for sundowners. Family-friendly until evening.

labrisa-bali.com →
💡 Book COMO Shambhala from Dubai. Escape Haven is 2 min walk — use whenever nanny is on.
🏆 Best in Canggu

COMO Shambhala

⏱ 10am–7pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.9

World-class. Balinese massage, deep tissue, facials, steam room. Couples from IDR 3,200,000 (~AED 760). Book well in advance from Dubai — fills fast.

comohotels.com →
On Your Street

Escape Haven

⏱ Open 24/7📍 2 min WALK⭐ 4.9

Literally on your street at No.85D. WhatsApp for a spontaneous massage whenever the nanny is on. Perfect for unplanned wellness moments at any hour.

escapehaven.com →

Fajar Bali Spa

⏱ 9am–11pm📍 4 min⭐ 4.9

4,637 reviews. Set among rice fields. Known for four-handed massage (two therapists simultaneously), hot stone and facials. One of the highest-rated spas in Canggu.

fajarbalispa.com →

The Present Spa

⏱ 10am–6pm📍 4 min⭐ 4.9

Specialises in Japanese-style head spa — scalp assessment, hot water ritual, extraordinary head/neck/shoulder massage. Consistently rated best Bali massage by couples.

thepresentspa.com →

ADDA Yoga · Villa Sessions

⏱ 7am–10pm📍 Comes to you⭐ 4.9

Instructor comes to your villa. Vinyasa, hatha, yin, restorative, meditation and breathwork. No studio trip needed — perfect with morning coffee by the pool.

addayogabali.com →

ZIN Wellness

⏱ 9am–9pm📍 12 min⭐ 4.9

Stunning spa. Balinese massage and facial packages. One client booked three more treatments the same day. Aromatherapy massage + natural facial combo is outstanding.

zin.world →
🧘 Also consider: Samadi Wellness (4 min walk), GOLDUST SPA (6 min), Spring Spa (value pick from IDR 310k). Ask your driver for their village massage recommendation — IDR 100–120k/hr (~AED 24).
📞 Book Muda by/Suka and Gente from Dubai — they fill up fast. All others are walk-in or easy same-day WhatsApp.
#1 Pick

Muda by/Suka

⏱ 4:30–11pm📍 6 min⭐ 4.9

Asian fusion at its finest. Wagyu ribeye, Lombok oysters, mushroom dumplings. The best restaurant in Canggu. Book from Dubai at bysuka.com — fills up fast.

bysuka.com →

Barboa Bali

⏱ 7am–9pm📍 2 min WALK⭐ 4.9

On your street! Avocado burrata toast, coconut chia pudding, excellent smoothies. Rated #1 café on your block. The perfect start to every morning.

barboabali.com →

Gente Italian Seafood

⏱ 5–11pm📍 10 min⭐ 4.9

Top Italian in Canggu. Owner-run, intimate. Outstanding octopus and prawn risotto. Book ahead from Dubai — one of the most loved restaurants in the area.

gentebali.com →
Dubai Favourite

Sababa

⏱ 11am–midnight📍 12 min⭐ 4.8

Middle Eastern and Moroccan. Slow-cooked lamb shank, falafel, chicken skewer. Moroccan décor, intimate atmosphere. Will feel like home after a week in Bali.

sabababali.com →

Beach Boy Canggu

⏱ Noon–11pm📍 6 min⭐ 4.9

Most underrated restaurant in Canggu. Exceptional food, great value, warm and attentive service. A local favourite that over-delivers on every visit.

beachboycanggu.com →

Warung Sika

⏱ 9am–9pm📍 5 min⭐ 4.6

Best nasi campur in Canggu. 30+ freshly cooked sides. A full plate ~IDR 49,000 (~AED 12). Does takeaway too — one of the best things you can eat here.

La Baracca

⏱ 11:30am–2am📍 6 min⭐ 4.6

Best pizza in Canggu. Neapolitan thin crust, wood-fired. Rooftop seating with views. Also great for takeaway — order via GoJek for a perfect villa night in.

labaraccabali.com →

Sa'Mesa

⏱ 6–11pm📍 4 min⭐ 4.8

18-course Italian surprise menu with free-flowing limoncello. Fun communal tables, very good food — and it's practically on your doorstep. Great for a fun group evening.

samesabali.com →
🍳 Also worth trying: Two Face Coffee (mango smoothie bowl), Santanera (Spanish tapas), Indigo (Japanese omakase), Lulu Bistrot (French, chandelier-lit), Vore (premium steak grill).

Pepito Market

⏱ 7am–midnight📍 4 min⭐ 4.4

The go-to expat supermarket. Imported cheese, wine, deli, organic produce. Do a big first-day shop here to stock the villa kitchen. Card accepted.

La Brisa Sunday Market

⏱ Sundays 10am–4pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.1

Boutique market at La Brisa beach club. Local fashion, organic beauty products, artisan food, crafts. A fun Sunday morning outing for the whole family.

Jl. Batu Bolong Strip

⏱ Daytime📍 5–10 min

The main shopping strip. Independent boutiques, surf shops, local fashion brands and homewares. Best explored in the morning before heat peaks. Great for Bali keepsakes.

GOLDUST SPA Boutique

⏱ 9am–9pm📍 6 min⭐ 4.7

Beautiful boutique hidden at the spa entrance. Curated clothing, accessories and homewares worth browsing even without a treatment booked.

goldustspa.com →

Pasar Desa Canggu

⏱ 6am–2pm📍 8 min⭐ 4.3

Traditional local market. Go before 9am for freshest tropical fruit, veg, tofu, tempeh and fish. A fraction of supermarket prices — bring cash, bring curiosity.

Currency

💰 IDR

Indonesian Rupiah. Quick rule of thumb: IDR 100,000 ≈ AED 24 ≈ USD 6.50. Withdraw at airport or Circle K ATMs. Most restaurants and spas accept card.

Must-Have Apps

📱 Download before leaving Dubai

GoJek — Bali's Careem. Rides, food delivery, GoSend. Register your number before departure. GoDrinks — wine and alcohol delivered to your villa in 30 mins. Google Maps — works perfectly across Bali. WhatsApp — everything runs on it.

Dedicated Driver

🚗 Book through villa host

With a toddler and nanny, hire a dedicated driver for all 5 villa days. They know the best warungs, handle parking and carry bags. Rate: IDR 600–800k/day (~AED 145–190), fuel included.

Book from Dubai

Nanny Services

👶 Book 4–6 weeks ahead

Feby'lous Bali Nanny (febylousbali.co.id) · Bali's Best Babysitting (balisbestbabysitting.com) · Karsini Services · Bali Nanny Hire. Request the same nanny every day — essential for a 2.5 year old's comfort.

Weather & Timing

☀️ Dry Season

May is the start of the dry season — warm and sunny 28–32°C with occasional brief showers. Far less crowded than July–August peak. Bali is 4 hours ahead of Dubai — minimal jet lag expected.

Alcohol Delivery

🍷 To the villa

GoDrinks (godrinks.com) — excellent wine, delivered cold in 30 mins. Drink Bottle Shop — free delivery in Canggu. Wow Booze Bali — 24/7 for late-night restocks.
